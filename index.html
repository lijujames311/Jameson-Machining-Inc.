Our Grinding Services

Cylindrical Grinding

We specialize in precision shaft grinding, but our capabilities go beyond — we can grind parts up to 24 inches in diameter and 80 inches in length, including internal (ID/IID) grinding. Whether it’s chrome, custom jobs, or challenging geometries, our equipment is built for it. We've invested in a full range of custom and pre-made grinding fixtures, allowing us to deliver repeatable accuracy without the need for customer-supplied tooling. Combined with our use of high-performance carbon, diamond, and other premium grinding wheels, we ensure consistent, high-quality results across a wide variety of applications.

Surface Grinding

Our surface grinding services focus on achieving flatness and tight surface finish tolerances. Backed by our investment in precision fixturing and top-grade grinding wheels, we deliver clean, consistent finishes even on challenging tool steels or specialty components. Our pre-made fixture setups ensure we can handle one-offs or production work efficiently — without delays or compromise in quality.

Centerless Grinding

We also offer limited centerless grinding capabilities through our German-engineered centerless belt grinding system. Ideal for light production and cleanup work on round parts, it provides smooth, accurate finishes where traditional center-type setups aren’t required.
