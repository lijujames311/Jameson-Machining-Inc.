Our Grinding Services

Cylindrical Grinding

We specialize in precision shaft grinding, but our capabilities go beyond — we can grind parts up to 24 inches in diameter and 80 inches in length, including internal (ID/IID) grinding. We use pre-made and custom fixtures, carbon, diamond, and other premium wheels to ensure consistent, high-quality results.

Surface Grinding

We achieve tight surface finish tolerances using precision fixturing and top-grade grinding wheels. Our setup allows for efficient one-off and small production grinding jobs without delays.

Centerless Grinding

We offer limited centerless grinding using a German-engineered belt grinder, ideal for cleanup and light production of round parts with smooth, accurate finishes.
